Hi. I'm a UX designer based in New Zealand.

I have a working history based on listening to and understanding people - what they want, how they can get there, where the hurdles are. I began as an artist with a Bachelor of Fine Arts, and luck has taken me around the world and into the IT industry where I learnt the foundation of my role was asking the right questions and learning what the user actually wanted. All my experience has brought me to the point of realising that user experience is completely dependent on successful design - of everything. Transistioning into web design allows me to bring all my life experiences together to apply them professionally.
